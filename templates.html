## SNON Templates SNON templates are collections of SNON definitions that are packaged up for distribution to SNON systems. Once loaded into an SNON system, instances of devices described by the template can be instantiated. New device SNON fragments are created for each corresponding device-template fragment 2. New sensor SNON fragments are created for each corresponding sensor-template fragment 3. New IDs are allocated for each messageID and entityID 4. precedentID fields are added or updated to point to the corresponding template fragment entityID 5. entityRelations fields are updated to point to the newly allocated IDs 6. Inherited fields are removed 7. By loading in an updated template, the newer fragments with the corresponding entityID are inherited from, instead of the older fragments. Additions and/or removals of sensors or devices to a template are more complicated, and requires enumerating all devices that inherit from the template, then performing a corresponding addition or removal operation. If inheritance from a template is not desired, all fields can be copied over, and precedentIDs are not added or changed.
